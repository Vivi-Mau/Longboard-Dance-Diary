## EN

Carving from side to side.

### Steps
- Legs a little more than shoulder width on the board, horizontal to the driving direction.
- Move your body weight from one side to another.

## DE

Carving

### Schritte
- Verlagere dein Gewicht von einer Seite zur anderen.
